First Choice Financial is among Western Canada’s leading non-bank mortgage lenders focusing on short term mortgages.
  • We are experts in:
    • construction mortgages for residential (single family and multi-family), commercial, and industrial projects; ranging from $500,000 to $20 million;
    • acquisition financing for real estate properties that require stabilization (i.e. lease up, improvements, repositioning, etc.);
    • land acquisition, and land development financing.
  • We primarily fund first mortgages, but we will consider second mortgages.
  • Since 2009 we have financed in excess of $500 million of real estate; and our mortgage portfolio is currently in excess of $125 million.
  • We also specialize in hotel financing across Canada; including construction financing for hotels and hotels outside major urban centres.
